What is PPC Advertising?

PPC (Pay-Per-Click) advertising is a highly effective form of digital marketing where advertisers pay only when a user clicks on their ad. These ads, often labeled as “sponsored” or “ad,” appear in search engine results or on websites. It is a powerful method to drive targeted traffic to your business and generate immediate results.
