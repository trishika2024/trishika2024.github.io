Sales Executive Job Delhi

📍 Location: Om Vihar, Uttam Nagar, New Delhi
👥 Open Positions: 1
🏢 Type: Full Time
🕑 Date Posted: Feb. 17, 2025, 12:04 p.m.

Job Overview

We are seeking a Sales Executive who will be responsible for generating sales and driving revenue growth. This role involves identifying new business opportunities, building relationships with potential clients, and managing the sales process. The ideal candidate will be passionate about sales, customer service, and achieving targets. Proficiency in both English and Hindi is required to effectively communicate with clients.

Job Responsibilities for Sales Executive

Here are the key duties and responsibilities for the Sales Executive role:

  • Identify and develop new business opportunities in the market.
  • Build and maintain strong relationships with clients and prospects.
  • Present and demonstrate products/services to potential clients.
  • Negotiate and close sales agreements with clients.
  • Achieve sales targets and contribute to business growth.
  • Manage the complete sales cycle from prospecting to closing.
  • Prepare and deliver sales presentations, proposals, and reports.
  • Follow up with clients post-sale to ensure customer satisfaction.
  • Stay updated with market trends and competitor activities.

Sales Executive Job Qualifications

  • 10th/12th pass (higher education will be an advantage).
  • Proven experience in sales or related roles (preferred).
  • Strong communication skills in English and Hindi (both written and verbal).
  • Good understanding of sales principles and techniques.
  • Ability to build rapport with clients and negotiate effectively.
  • Proficiency in MS Office and CRM software.

Sales Executive Skills Required

  • Excellent sales and negotiation skills.
  • Strong customer service and relationship-building abilities.
  • Goal-oriented and results-driven.
  • Good time management and organizational skills.
  • Ability to work under pressure and meet sales targets.
  • Self-motivated and proactive in seeking new sales opportunities.

What to Expect as a Sales Executive

  • You will work in a dynamic sales environment.
  • Regular interaction with potential clients and teams.
  • Achievement of sales targets will be a key focus of the role.
  • Opportunities for career growth and commission-based incentives.

Sales Executive Salaries

The average salary for a Sales Executive at Trishika Solutions is ₹8,000 – ₹12,000 per month, with additional incentives based on sales performance.
Salary may vary depending on experience, responsibilities, and sales targets.
