Job Overview

As a Digital Marketing Executive, you will be responsible for running various marketing campaigns across multiple platforms, allocating budgets and timelines, and ensuring successful execution. You will analyze campaign performance and optimize strategies to maximize customer engagement. Additionally, you will update and maintain website and social media content to enhance brand visibility and customer awareness.

Job Responsibilities for Digital Marketing Executive

Here is a list of the duties for which a digital marketing executive will be responsible:

  • Plan and execute SEO, web, social media, email, and display marketing campaigns.
  • Allocate budgets strategically and schedule campaigns efficiently.
  • Collect, analyze, and interpret data from marketing campaigns.
  • Identify trends and communicate insights to relevant teams.
  • Track and measure conversion rates from digital marketing efforts.
  • Optimize customer conversion points to enhance the user funnel.
  • Stay updated with emerging marketing technologies to improve performance.
  • Regularly measure and evaluate digital campaign performance against KPIs.
  • Brainstorm new digital marketing strategies to engage the target audience.
  • Create, proofread, and edit digital content to maintain brand consistency.

Digital Marketing Executive Job Qualifications

Here is a list of qualifications that hiring directors look for when hiring a digital marketing executive:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Business, Communications, or related fields.
  • Prior experience in digital marketing or relevant fields.
  • Strong understanding of SEO, marketing databases, email, and social media marketing.
  • Experience managing websites and using marketing analytics tools like Google Analytics, WebTrends, or similar platforms.
  • Basic knowledge of HTML, JavaScript, Adobe Acrobat, and advertising tools is a plus.

Digital Marketing Executive Skills Required

Here is a list of skills that will be helpful for the daily responsibilities of a digital marketing executive:

  • Strong IT and data analysis skills.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Excel, PowerPoint).
  • Goal-driven and customer-focused approach.
  • Excellent project management skills, ability to meet deadlines, and multitask under pressure.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • Analytical mindset to assess and optimize digital campaigns.
  • Ability to identify market opportunities and emerging trends.
  • Experience with marketing research and data analysis software.
  • Creative thinking to develop innovative marketing ideas.
  • Ability to present data visually using charts and reports.
  • Strong interpersonal skills to coordinate with teams and build client relationships.
  • Familiarity with CRM software, CMS, and new/existing social media platforms.

What to Expect as a Digital Marketing Executive

Here’s what it’s like to be a Digital Marketing Executive at Trishika Solutions:

  • Regular research and data analysis will be required throughout the year.
  • Working hours are generally stable but may vary based on campaign requirements.
  • Success rates in digital campaigns may vary, requiring adaptability and learning.
  • Handling multiple projects will require working on tight deadlines and multitasking.
  • Keeping up with daily market trends is crucial to maximize marketing impact.
